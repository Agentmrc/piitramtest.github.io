SOLVE COMBINED INEQUALITY (TYPE 1)


QUESTION: \(-2 ≤ \frac{3 - 5m}{4} < \frac{1}{3}\)


Explanation Solution
1. Find the common denominator \( -2(12) ≤ 3(3-5m) < 4 \)
2. Solve and simplify the expression \( -24 ≤ 9 - 15m < 4 \)
3. Get the answer in the interval form \(\frac{1}{3} < m ≤ \frac{33}{15} \)
